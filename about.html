About Our AI System

We developed an AI-powered Plant Disease Detection system that helps farmers and researchers identify plant diseases quickly using deep learning and image processing techniques.

🌿 Project Overview

This system uses Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) trained on the PlantVillage dataset. It analyzes leaf images and predicts disease type with confidence score.

🎯 Our Goal

To support agriculture by providing fast, accurate, and easy-to-use AI tools that help detect plant diseases early and improve crop health.
